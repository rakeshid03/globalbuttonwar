# 🌍 Global Button War

A massive real-time worldwide button pressing battle where countries compete for the #1 spot on the global leaderboard.

Players from around the world can press the giant button once every 24 hours to help their country climb the rankings and dominate the world stage.

## 🚀 Play Now

[`🔴 PRESS`](https://rakeshid03.github.io/globalbuttonwar/) the global button and represent your country in the ultimate international click war.

## ✨ Features

- 🌎 Real-time worldwide country leaderboard
- ⏰ One press allowed every 24 hours
- 🏆 Live global rankings
- 📡 Real-time synchronization using Gun.js
- 🎨 Unlock global button color changes
- 🌙 Modern glassmorphism interface
- 🎵 Interactive sound effects
- ⚡ Fast lightweight browser gameplay
- 📊 Live activity feed
- 🌍 Automatic country detection
- ☁️ No registration required

## 🕹️ How To Play
1. Open the game.
2. Your country is detected automatically.
3. Press the giant button.
4. Wait 24 hours before pressing again.
5. Help your country climb the leaderboard.
6. Maintain streaks to unlock special rewards.
7. Become the world's strongest button nation.

## 🏆 Achievements

- 👆 First Click
- 🔥 3 Day Streak
- 🥇 Top Supporter
- 🎨 Color Master
- 🎖️ Veteran Player

## 🎨 Color Change Pass

Reach a 3-day streak to unlock the exclusive **Color Change Pass**.

Use it to change the global button color for every player around the world instantly.
